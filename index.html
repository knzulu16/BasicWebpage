    Historic places in the V&A Waterfront


    Clock Tower


  1. Clock Tower has always been an icon of old docks.
  2. It is where the Port Captain could check on all the activities in the harbor.
  3. It can be accessed from the greater Waterfront via a pedestrian swing bridge, which opens and closes periodically to allow boats through.

    African Trading Port

  1. Artwork from practically each African country and culture is available.
  2. Collect available artwork from rural areas and local communities.
  3. Attracting many tourists.

    Swing Bridge

  • The bridge swings over the link between the Alfred Basin and the Victoria Basin in the Victoria & Alfred Waterfront.
  • It was designed and constructed in 1997.
  • Bridge was built to give visitors and pedestrians easy access.
  • Easy access to people with disabilities.
